Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

The D3 interactive scatterplot above allows us to click through multiple variables to see if there is relationship between the varibles for each state in the USA

The plots show that:

  • There seems to be a positive correlation in the states between lack of healthcare and poverty, with Massachusetts having the best healthcare coverage,while New Hampshire has the lowest poverty rate.
  • The median age in the sample surveyed is within 30-45.
  • The correlation between the percentage of people who smoke and the percentage of people living in poverty is positive.
